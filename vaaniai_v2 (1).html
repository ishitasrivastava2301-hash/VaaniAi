The Problem

The tools exist.
They just weren't built for here.

Walk into any kirana in Bengaluru and the owner knows your name, your usual order, your mother's medicine schedule. Their phone support? A bot that says "I didn't understand that. Please repeat."

01

The bot speaks the wrong language

A Kannada customer calls a local pharmacy. The IVR says "Press 1 for English." She presses nothing. She hangs up. That's a lost sale.

02

Every call starts from zero

She called last Tuesday about the same delayed order. The bot has no idea. She has to repeat everything again. This is what "support" feels like for most Indian customers.

03

Small businesses can't hire their way out

A 4-person medical store isn't going to hire a support team. So calls go to voicemail. Customers don't leave voicemails. They leave and don't come back.

04

Existing bots can't think

"Kal mera order nahi aaya bhai" doesn't match any keyword in the IVR menu. The caller gets routed to billing. She hangs up again.

"Raju has run his pharmacy in Dharwad for 11 years.
He knows every customer by name. His support tool doesn't know any of them."

Our Solution

Not a smarter chatbot.
An agent that actually does things.

VaaniAI picks up the call, understands what the customer actually said (in whatever language they said it), fixes the problem if it can, and tells the owner what happened. That's it.

🗣️

Understands how Indians actually speak

Not just Hindi or English — but the mix. "Bhai mera order nahi aaya, 3 din ho gaye" is understood perfectly. No language menu, no repeating yourself.

⚡

Fixes things, doesn't just say sorry

Order delayed? It checks the status, activates priority shipping, and confirms — all in the same 90-second call. No human needed for 80% of issues.

🧠

Remembers every caller, every time

The second time Priya calls, VaaniAI already knows her name, her preferred language, and that she had a delayed order last week. It feels less like a bot and more like a person.

📲

Owner gets a WhatsApp summary after every call

Ravi doesn't need to listen to recordings. He gets a clean message: what happened, what was resolved, what needs his attention. Takes 10 seconds to read.

HOW IT WORKS
Caller speaks
Amazon Connect receives the call. Transcribe converts speech to text — including Hinglish and code-mixing.
Memory loaded
DynamoDB profile fetched instantly — name, language, order history, past issues.
Agent reasons & acts
Bedrock Agent queries the Knowledge Base, takes actions via Lambda — refunds, order lookups, escalation.
Reply + memory saved
Polly speaks the reply in the caller's language. Profile updated. WhatsApp summary sent to owner.

Tech Stack

Built on AWS,
chosen for a reason.

Each service does one thing well. Together they handle a full call — from ring to resolution — in under 2 seconds of latency.

📞
Amazon Connect
Handles the actual phone call. Routes it in, manages the session, hands off cleanly.
🎙️
Amazon Transcribe
Turns speech to text in real time — and actually handles Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Hinglish.
🧠
Amazon Bedrock
The reasoning layer. Understands intent, queries the knowledge base, decides what action to take.
📚
Bedrock Knowledge Bases
The business's FAQs, policies, and catalogue — searchable by the agent mid-call, no retraining needed.
⚡
AWS Lambda
Runs the real actions — checks orders, triggers refunds, escalates — all while the call is live.
🗄️
Amazon DynamoDB
Stores caller profiles. Retrieved in under 100ms at the start of every call so the agent already knows who's calling.
🔊
Amazon Polly
Speaks back in the caller's language. Neural voices — not the robotic ones you're thinking of.
📲
WhatsApp API
Sends the owner a plain-language summary after every call. No dashboard to log into.
